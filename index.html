02 — ABOUT

About the Symposium

LIVAZ 2K26 is a premier technical symposium organized by the Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering in association with the IEEE Student Branch at Sri Sairam College of Engineering. This dynamic event brings together students from diverse disciplines to showcase their technical expertise, creativity, and innovative ideas through a wide range of engaging activities. Participants will have the opportunity to compete, learn, and collaborate across both technical and non-technical domains, fostering knowledge exchange and professional growth.
